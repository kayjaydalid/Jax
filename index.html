JAX CAR RENTAL — RENTAL AGREEMENT

This Car Rental Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between JAX Car Rental ("Lessor") and the renter ("Lessee") identified in the booking form. This agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of the Philippines, including the Civil Code of the Philippines (Republic Act No. 386), the Land Transportation and Traffic Code (Republic Act No. 4136), and other applicable regulations.

1. RENTAL PERIOD & RATES

The rental period shall begin upon release of the vehicle and end upon its return. Rates are based on the destination area and duration (12-hour or 24-hour). Overtime charges of ₱150 per hour shall apply beyond the agreed rental period. The car wash fee of ₱200 is mandatory upon return.

2. ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

The Lessee must: (a) be at least 21 years of age; (b) possess a valid Philippine driver's license or, for foreigners, an International Driving Permit (IDP) alongside their foreign license, per LTO regulations; (c) present at least two (2) valid government-issued IDs; (d) provide a refundable security deposit as determined by the Lessor.

3. USE OF VEHICLE

The vehicle (Nissan Livina) shall be used exclusively for lawful purposes. The Lessee shall NOT: (a) use the vehicle for hire, racing, or any illegal activity; (b) drive under the influence of alcohol or prohibited substances (R.A. 10586 — Anti-Drunk and Drugged Driving Act); (c) allow unauthorized drivers to operate the vehicle; (d) take the vehicle outside the agreed destination without prior written consent; (e) use the vehicle for towing or pushing another vehicle.

4. FUEL POLICY

The vehicle shall be returned with the same fuel level as when received. If not, the Lessee shall pay the prevailing fuel cost plus a ₱200 refueling service charge.

5. INSURANCE & LIABILITY

Basic Comprehensive Third-Party Liability (CTPL) insurance as mandated by R.A. 10607 (Amended Insurance Code) is included. The Lessee shall be personally liable for: (a) damages resulting from negligence, recklessness, or violation of traffic laws; (b) loss of personal belongings left inside the vehicle; (c) towing costs and fines from traffic violations incurred during the rental period; (d) the insurance deductible amount in case of an accident claim.

6. ACCIDENT & INCIDENT REPORTING

In the event of any accident, breakdown, or theft, the Lessee must: (a) immediately notify JAX Car Rental at 09928176144; (b) file a police report at the nearest station within 24 hours; (c) NOT attempt unauthorized repairs; (d) cooperate fully with the Lessor's insurance claim process.

7. DAMAGE & PENALTIES

The Lessee shall be responsible for any damage to the vehicle not covered by insurance, including but not limited to: interior damage, missing accessories, tire damage, undercarriage damage. The cost of repairs shall be based on authorized dealer rates. Loss of rental income during repair period may also be charged.

8. CANCELLATION POLICY

Cancellations made 48 hours or more before the scheduled rental period shall receive a full refund of any advance payment. Cancellations within 24–48 hours shall incur a 50% cancellation fee. Cancellations less than 24 hours before shall forfeit the advance payment.

9. DELIVERY & PICK-UP

Delivery is available for ₱300. The vehicle must be returned to the agreed pick-up location. Failure to return the vehicle within 6 hours of the agreed return time without prior arrangement may be considered qualified theft under Article 310 of the Revised Penal Code.

10. DATA PRIVACY

In compliance with R.A. 10173 — Data Privacy Act of 2012, the Lessor shall collect, use, and store personal data solely for the purpose of processing the rental transaction and will not disclose information to third parties without consent, except as required by law.

11. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved through amicable settlement. If unresolved, the parties agree to submit to the jurisdiction of the proper courts in the Province of Bulacan, Philippines.

12. ACKNOWLEDGMENT

By submitting the booking form and checking the "I agree" box, the Lessee acknowledges having read, understood, and agreed to all terms and conditions set forth herein. This electronic agreement shall have the same legal effect as a signed physical document, in accordance with R.A. 8792 — E-Commerce Act of 2000.
